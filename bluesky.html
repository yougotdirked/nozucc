Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

Bluesky is built on the AT Protocol, which provides data portability:

  • Your data can potentially be exported, though the process may vary
  • Consider screenshotting important posts or conversations
  • Save any media files (photos, videos) you want to keep
  • Note: Native data export tools may be limited compared to older platforms

Important Considerations

  • Your username (handle) may become available for others to claim
  • All posts, replies, and likes will be permanently deleted
  • Your followers and following lists will be removed
  • Direct messages will be deleted
  • Any custom feeds you created will be removed

Deletion Steps (Mobile App)

  1. Open the Bluesky App
    Launch the Bluesky app on your iOS or Android device
  2. Access Settings
    Tap on your profile icon/avatar in the bottom navigation bar
    Tap the gear icon (‚öôÔ∏è) or "Settings" option
  3. Navigate to Account Settings
    Scroll down to find "Account" section
    Look for "Delete account" or "Deactivate account" option
  4. Initiate Deletion
    Tap on "Delete my account"
    Read the warning about permanent deletion
  5. Confirm Your Identity
    Enter your password to verify it's you
    You may need to type your handle or email for additional confirmation
  6. Final Confirmation
    Acknowledge that you understand the deletion is permanent
    Tap "Delete Account" or "Confirm" to finalize

Deletion Steps (Web Browser)

  1. Log in to Bluesky
    Visit bsky.app/settings and log in to your account
  2. Access Settings
    Click on your profile icon in the left sidebar or top navigation
    Click "Settings" from the menu
  3. Find Account Deletion
    Scroll down to the "Account" or "Danger Zone" section
    Look for "Delete my account" option
  4. Begin Deletion Process
    Click on "Delete my account"
    Review the information about what will be deleted
  5. Verify and Confirm
    Enter your password
    Optionally provide a reason for leaving
    Click the final confirmation button to delete

Alternative: Deactivate or Take a Break

If you're not ready for permanent deletion:

  • Simply stop using the app and don't log in
  • Remove the app from your devices to avoid temptation
  • Change your password to a random string and don't save it (effectively locking yourself out)
  • Note: Bluesky may not have a formal "deactivation" feature like other platforms

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your profile is immediately removed from Bluesky
  • All your posts (skeets) and replies are deleted
  • Your followers and following lists are removed
  • Your username/handle becomes available for others to use
  • Direct messages are deleted from your account
  • Custom feeds you created will no longer be accessible
  • Your data should be removed from Bluesky's servers
  • Some cached content may temporarily remain on other servers in the federation

Understanding the AT Protocol

Bluesky is built on the decentralized AT Protocol, which means:

  • Your data may exist on multiple servers (Personal Data Servers or PDS)
  • Deleting from the main Bluesky server should remove your data
  • If you used a custom PDS, you may need to manage deletion there separately
  • The protocol design aims to give users control over their data

Post-Deletion Steps

After deleting your account:

  • Remove the Bluesky app from your devices
  • Clear browser cookies and cache if you used the web version
  • Unsubscribe from any Bluesky emails
  • Consider alternatives like decentralized platforms or taking a social media break
