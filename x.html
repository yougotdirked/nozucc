How to Delete Your X (Twitter) Account

Permanently deactivate your X/Twitter account and escape the toxicity

⚠️ Important: When you deactivate your X account, it will be hidden for 30 days before permanent deletion. During this period, you can restore it by logging back in. After 30 days, your account and all data are permanently deleted.

Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

X allows you to download an archive of your tweets and data:

  • Go to Settings and privacy → Your account → Download an archive of your data
  • Click "Request archive"
  • Verify your password and complete any security checks
  • X will email you a link to download your archive (usually within 24 hours)
  • The archive includes tweets, messages, photos, videos, and more

Important Considerations

  • Your @username will become available for others to claim after deletion
  • All tweets, likes, and retweets will be permanently deleted
  • Direct messages you sent will be deleted from your account but may remain visible to recipients
  • Third-party apps using your X account will lose access
  • If you use X/Twitter for login on other websites, those connections will break

Deletion Steps (Web Browser)

  1. Log in to X
    Visit x.com (or twitter.com) and log in to your account
  2. Access Settings
    Click "More" (three dots icon) in the left sidebar
    Select "Settings and privacy"
  3. Navigate to Account Deactivation
    Click on "Your account"
    Scroll down and click "Deactivate your account"
  4. Read the Deactivation Information
    X will show you what happens when you deactivate
    Click "Deactivate" at the bottom of the page
  5. Confirm Your Password
    Enter your account password to verify it's you
    Click "Deactivate account"
  6. Final Confirmation
    Click "Yes, deactivate" on the confirmation popup
    Your account will be deactivated immediately

Deletion Steps (Mobile App)

  1. Open the X App
    Launch the X app on your iOS or Android device
  2. Access Settings
    Tap your profile icon
    Tap "Settings and privacy"
  3. Navigate to Account
    Tap "Your account"
    Scroll down and tap "Deactivate your account"
  4. Review and Confirm
    Read the deactivation information
    Tap "Deactivate"
  5. Enter Password
    Type your password to confirm
    Tap "Deactivate account"

The 30-Day Grace Period

What happens during the 30 days:

  • Your profile is immediately hidden from public view
  • Your tweets and account information are not visible to others
  • Your username and email remain reserved (cannot be used by others)
  • You can cancel the deletion by simply logging back in

After 30 days:

  • Your account and all data are permanently deleted
  • Your username becomes available for others to use
  • Recovery is not possible

What Happens After Deletion?

  • All your tweets, retweets, and likes are permanently removed
  • Your followers and following lists are deleted
  • Your profile and username disappear from X
  • Direct messages are removed from your account
  • Some content may remain in search engines or archives temporarily
  • Third-party services that displayed your tweets will no longer have access
  • Cached versions may exist temporarily on search engines

Removing Your Digital Footprint

After deleting your account, consider:

  • Asking search engines to remove cached versions of your profile
  • Contacting third-party services that may have archived your tweets
  • Clearing your browser cookies and cache
  • Removing X from any apps that used it for authentication
  • Unsubscribing from X emails if you receive any
Note: If you have X Premium (formerly Twitter Blue), make sure to cancel your subscription before deactivating to avoid being charged. Go to Settings → Premium → Manage subscription.
