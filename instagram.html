How to Delete Your Instagram Account

Permanently remove your Instagram account and all associated data

Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

If you want to keep a copy of your photos, videos, and other data:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Your Activity ‚Üí Download Your Information
  • Select the data you want to download
  • Choose format (HTML or JSON) and media quality
  • Instagram will email you a link when your data is ready (can take up to 48 hours)

Deletion Steps

Note: You cannot delete your Instagram account from the mobile app. You must use a web browser.

  1. Open a Web Browser
    Go to instagram.com/accounts/remove/request/permanent/
  2. Log In
    Enter your Instagram username and password to access the deletion page
  3. Select Reason for Deletion
    Choose a reason from the dropdown menu (this is required)
  4. Re-enter Your Password
    Instagram will ask you to confirm your password for security
  5. Click "Permanently Delete My Account"
    This button will appear in red. Click it to initiate the deletion process
  6. 30-Day Grace Period
    Your account will be deactivated immediately and scheduled for permanent deletion in 30 days. If you log in during this period, the deletion will be cancelled

Alternative: Temporarily Disable Your Account

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, you can temporarily disable your account:

  1. Log in to Instagram via a web browser
  2. Go to your profile and click "Edit Profile"
  3. Scroll down and click "Temporarily disable my account" in the bottom right
  4. Select a reason and re-enter your password
  5. Click "Temporarily Disable Account"
üí° Tip: When your account is temporarily disabled, your profile, photos, comments, and likes will be hidden until you reactivate it by logging back in.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your profile, photos, videos, comments, likes, and followers will be permanently removed
  • Your username will become available for others to use
  • You won't be able to sign up again with the same username
  • Direct messages you sent will still be visible to recipients
  • Some data may remain on Instagram's backup servers for a limited time
