How to Delete Your Facebook Account

Permanently remove your Facebook account and reclaim your privacy

Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

Facebook allows you to download a copy of your data:

  • Click the dropdown arrow in the top right ‚Üí Settings & Privacy ‚Üí Settings
  • Go to "Your Facebook Information" ‚Üí "Download Your Information"
  • Select the date range, format, and media quality
  • Click "Create File" and Facebook will notify you when it's ready (can take several hours or days)

Important Considerations

  • If you use Facebook Login for other apps/websites, you'll lose access to them
  • Your Messenger account will also be deleted
  • If you admin any Facebook Pages or Groups, you must transfer ownership or delete them first
  • Your Oculus/Meta Quest device may be affected if linked to your Facebook account

Deletion Steps

  1. Log in to Facebook
    Visit facebook.com and log in to your account
  2. Access Settings
    Click the dropdown arrow in the top right corner
    Select "Settings & Privacy" ‚Üí "Settings"
  3. Navigate to Account Deletion
    Click "Your Facebook Information" in the left sidebar
    Click "Deactivation and Deletion"
  4. Choose "Delete Account"
    Select "Permanently Delete Account" (not deactivate)
    Click "Continue to Account Deletion"
  5. Confirm Deletion
    Enter your password
    Complete any security checks if prompted
    Click "Continue"
  6. Final Confirmation
    Click "Delete Account" to confirm
    Your account will be scheduled for permanent deletion in 30 days

Direct Deletion Link

You can also go directly to: facebook.com/help/delete_account

Alternative: Deactivate Your Account

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, you can temporarily deactivate:

  1. Follow steps 1-3 above to reach "Deactivation and Deletion"
  2. Select "Deactivate Account" instead of delete
  3. Choose how long you want to stay deactivated (optional)
  4. Click "Continue" and follow the prompts
üí° Tip: When deactivated, your profile is hidden but you can reactivate anytime by logging back in. Your data is still stored on Facebook's servers.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • 30-Day Grace Period: Your account is scheduled for deletion but you can cancel by logging in
  • After 30 Days: Your account and all data are permanently deleted
  • Some information may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days
  • Messages you've sent to others may still be visible to them
  • Some logs and data may be retained for legal and security purposes
  • You cannot create a new account with the same email immediately

Removing Facebook Tracking

Even after deleting your account, Facebook may still track you across the web. Consider:

  • Installing browser extensions like Facebook Container (Firefox) or uBlock Origin
  • Clearing your browser cookies and cache
  • Opting out of Facebook's off-platform tracking at facebook.com/off_facebook_activity
