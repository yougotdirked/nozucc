How to Delete Your LinkedIn Account

Permanently close your LinkedIn account and protect your professional data

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Closing your LinkedIn account is permanent. You will lose access to your connections, messages, recommendations, endorsements, and all content you've posted. LinkedIn may retain some data for legal and business purposes.

Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

LinkedIn allows you to request an archive of your data:

  • Click on "Me" icon in the top right ‚Üí Settings & Privacy
  • Go to "Data privacy" ‚Üí "Get a copy of your data"
  • Select the data you want to download
  • Click "Request archive"
  • LinkedIn will email you when your archive is ready (usually within 24 hours)

Important Considerations

  • All your professional connections will be lost
  • Job applications made through LinkedIn may be affected
  • Recommendations and endorsements you've given will be removed
  • Content you've posted in groups will remain but won't be associated with your name
  • Your profile will no longer appear in search results

Deletion Steps

  1. Log in to LinkedIn
    Visit linkedin.com and sign in to your account
  2. Access Settings
    Click on your profile picture/icon in the top right corner
    Select "Settings & Privacy" from the dropdown menu
  3. Navigate to Account Management
    Click on the "Account preferences" section in the left sidebar
    Look for "Account management" at the bottom
  4. Select "Closing your LinkedIn account"
    Click on the link that says "Change" next to "Closing your LinkedIn account"
  5. Choose Reason for Leaving
    Select a reason from the list (required)
    Optionally provide additional feedback
  6. Enter Your Password
    For security verification, enter your account password
  7. Confirm Closure
    Check the confirmation box acknowledging you understand the consequences
    Click "Close account" or "Next" to finalize

Direct Link Method

You can also access the account closure page directly:

  • Go to: linkedin.com/psettings/account-management
  • Scroll to "Closing your LinkedIn account" and follow the steps above

Alternative: Hibernate Your Account

LinkedIn offers a "hibernation" mode that hides your profile without deleting it:

  1. Go to Settings & Privacy ‚Üí Account preferences ‚Üí Hibernation
  2. Toggle on "Hibernate account"
  3. Your profile will be hidden from search and your network
  4. You can reactivate anytime by logging back in
üí° Note: Hibernation keeps your data stored on LinkedIn's servers. It's only hidden from public view and can be reactivated at any time.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your profile is immediately removed from LinkedIn
  • Your name and profile information will no longer appear in search results
  • All your connections, messages, and endorsements are deleted
  • Content you've posted may remain visible for a short time in caches
  • LinkedIn may retain some data for legal compliance and business purposes
  • You can create a new account later, but you'll start from scratch
  • Your email address will be available for creating a new account after some time

After Closing Your Account

Consider these steps for complete removal:

  • Unsubscribe from LinkedIn emails using links in any emails you receive
  • Remove LinkedIn from any apps that use it for authentication
  • Clear your browser cookies and cache
  • Check if recruiters or colleagues have copies of your profile/resume
For Premium Subscribers: Cancel your Premium subscription before closing your account to avoid being charged. Go to Settings & Privacy ‚Üí Account preferences ‚Üí Subscriptions & payments.
