Before You Delete

Download Your Data (Optional)

  • Tap "Account" ‚Üí "Download your data"

  • TikTok will notify you when your data file is ready (up to 4 days)
  • Download the file within 4 days of receiving notification

Important Considerations

  • All your videos will be permanently deleted after 30 days
  • Comments, likes, and followers will be removed
  • Your username will become available for others to claim
  • You'll lose access to TikTok Creator Fund if enrolled
  • Direct messages will be deleted from your account
  • Videos you posted may still exist in duets, stitches, or downloads by others

Deletion Steps (Mobile App)

Note: Account deletion can only be done through the TikTok mobile app, not via web browser. You can visit tiktok.com for more information.

  1. Open the TikTok App
    Launch TikTok on your iOS or Android device
  2. Go to Your Profile
    Tap the "Profile" icon (person icon) in the bottom right corner
  3. Access Settings
    Tap the three horizontal lines (‚ò∞) in the top right corner
    Tap "Settings and privacy"
  4. Navigate to Account Management
    Tap "Account"
    Scroll down to find "Delete account"
  5. Review Deletion Information
    TikTok will explain what happens when you delete
    Tap "Continue" to proceed
  6. Verify Your Identity
    Enter your password, or verify via SMS code
    Complete any additional security verification if prompted
  7. Select a Reason
    Choose a reason for deleting (optional but may be required)
    Tap "Continue"
  8. Final Confirmation
    Read the final warning about the 30-day deletion period
    Tap "Delete account" to confirm
    Your account enters deactivation mode immediately

The 30-Day Deactivation Period

During the 30 days:

  • Your account is deactivated and hidden from public view
  • Your profile and videos are not visible to others
  • You can cancel the deletion by logging back in
  • Your data is scheduled for permanent deletion

After 30 days:

  • Your account is permanently deleted
  • All data is removed from TikTok's servers
  • Recovery is impossible
  • Your username becomes available for new users

Alternative: Temporarily Disable Your Account

TikTok also offers a temporary disable option:

  1. Follow steps 1-4 above to reach Account settings
  2. Instead of "Delete account," look for "Manage account"
  3. Select "Temporarily disable account"
  4. Choose a time period (7, 14, or 30 days)
  5. Verify and confirm
  6. Your account will be hidden but can be reactivated by logging in

What Happens After Deletion?

  • All your videos are permanently deleted after 30 days
  • Your profile disappears from TikTok
  • Comments and likes you made are removed
  • Your followers and following lists are deleted
  • Direct messages are removed from your account
  • Your username becomes available for new accounts
  • Videos you're featured in (duets, stitches) remain but without your profile link
  • Videos others downloaded will still exist on their devices
  • Some analytics data may be retained for business purposes

Dealing with TikTok Addiction

If you're deleting TikTok due to excessive use:

  • Remove the app from all your devices immediately
  • Block TikTok website on your browsers
  • Find alternative activities for your free time
  • Consider using screen time management apps
  • Tell friends/family for accountability
  • Replace scrolling with reading, hobbies, or exercise

Privacy After Deletion

Important Privacy Notes:

  • TikTok is owned by ByteDance, a Chinese company
  • The app has been criticized for extensive data collection
  • After deletion, remove the app completely from your devices
  • Clear cache and cookies from browsers where you used TikTok
  • Consider reviewing what data TikTok collected before deletion
  • Some data may be retained for legal/business purposes

Additional Steps After Deletion

  • Uninstall the TikTok app from all devices
  • Remove TikTok from any connected accounts (Google, Apple, Facebook)
  • Unsubscribe from TikTok emails
  • Clear app cache and data before uninstalling
  • Review privacy settings on other apps that may have TikTok integration
